Allan Pettersson

Swedish Composer

Home - Skånegatan 87

Allan Pettersson grew up in Skånegatan 87 (near Vita bergen park), Södermalm, Stockholm.

Home - Åsögatan 127

For thirty years, Allan Pettersson and his wife Gudrun lived in two rooms in the fourth floor of an old apartment building in Åsögatan 127, Södermalm, Stockholm..

Home - Bastugatan 30

In October 1976, both moved to State living quarters in Södermalm's Bastugatan 30.
